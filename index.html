About Me

As a Grade 12 STEM Health Allied student, I’ve always had a deep passion for science and technology, which naturally led me to the field of healthcare. From a young age, the advancements of medicine, especially in dental care, have inspired me. I am particularly drawn to the way technology is constantly shaping and improving dental treatments. Though my focus has always been on dentistry, I am also open to exploring other fields within healthcare, as I’m eager to discover all the ways I can positively impact people’s lives.

My ultimate goal is to become an orthodontist. I firmly believe that a healthy, confident smile can transform a person’s life. This belief drives my desire to specialize in orthodontics, where I can help individuals achieve the smile they deserve. As I continue my academic journey, I am dedicated to gaining the knowledge and skills necessary to thrive in this field, and I look forward to the challenges and opportunities that lie ahead.

Outside of academics, I find inspiration in music, poetry, and reading. These creative outlets provide me with a sense of balance and clarity, allowing me to recharge and reflect. Writing, in particular, is a form of self-expression that I hold dear. It allows me to communicate my thoughts and connect with others on a deeper level, helping me grow both personally and creatively.

Additionally, I have a strong passion for design. I enjoy creating posters, presentations, and covers, as it gives me the chance to merge creativity with functionality. Whether for school projects or personal expression, design allows me to visually communicate ideas in a way that words alone cannot. I hope to continue using these skills in the future, whether within the medical field or in other creative avenues.

Ultimately, my goal is to make a meaningful difference in the lives of others. I am confident that my passion for dentistry, along with my creativity and dedication, will help me contribute to the well-being of those I serve.

Research/Development

CureCup Innovation

“ Sip the cure, skip the frown, with our cup, healing is found!”

CureCup Innovation’s Medisip is a groundbreaking product designed to make medication intake easier for children. This eco-friendly tumbler features separate compartments for water and medicine, allowing parents to dilute medication and offer it to children without the unpleasant taste. With a built-in timer, Medisip ensures timely medication administration, while its fun, colorful design appeals to kids, making it an effective solution for families struggling with medication adherence. Medisip is made from durable stainless steel, providing an environmentally friendly alternative to plastic. Targeting parents of children aged 4 to 11, Medisip aims to improve the medicine-taking experience worldwide, combining convenience, safety, and sustainability.
